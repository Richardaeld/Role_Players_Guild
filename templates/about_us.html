Welcome Friends & Family!

This guild is being built for all those that enjoy the act of role play. From beginner to skilled, join our ranks and let us all benefit from your view point. We expect everyone to be respectful and encouraging. We can roleplay and discuss evil but evil or hatful commits will not be tolerated on our forms. We can also discuss WOTC content and how you the player and DM choose to roleplay it and bring it to life; however, please do not directly post source material. Due to legal reasons, that cannot be allowed.

We hope to have a good time and improve not only everyone that joins us, but ourselves as well. So, please join us on our journey to aspire to a greater level of immersion and skill through developing a caring and helpful community of like minded individuals. We will share our tricks and thoughts and hope you will do the same through our interactive forms. We are constructing a number of different guild tables that are devoted to various champaigns, settings, and ideas. Currently the Role Players Guild has three major halls. These halls are, World Building, Campaigns, and WOTC Campaigns where we focus on making our own worlds more vibrant and real.

World Building

Here we focus on building our auxiliary ideas. From alchemy to religion having these extras pulls our players in. Have a module you cannot play without. Recommend it here! The devils in the details or maybe its just a toothy mimic. Tell us about it here!

Campaigns

Love it, hate it, a DM's personal build world is a tribute to their dedication to the game and their players. Here we discuss how a DM brings their world to life. With a heavy focus on the elements that bring the experience from an idea to a enthralling experience

WOTC Campaigns

All things cannon are discussed here. What experiences brought this official campaign to life for DM and players. Was there extra material a DM incorporated thats truly adds to the experience? Maybe something they choose to omit or alter because of a mistake or refusal to wipe the party.
